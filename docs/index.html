What are my rights if I am arrested?
Under the Code of Criminal Procedure, you have the right to be informed of the grounds of arrest, the right to consult a lawyer of your choice, and the right to be produced before a magistrate within 24 hours.

Can I get bail?
Yes. Bail depends on whether the offence is bailable or non-bailable. For bailable offences, bail is a right. For non-bailable offences, it is at the court's discretion.
